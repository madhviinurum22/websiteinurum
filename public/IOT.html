MQTT:

MQTT Tester is a powerful yet user-friendly mobile application designed for developers and MQTT enthusiasts alike. Whether you’re testing MQTT-based IoT devices, debugging MQTT protocols, or simply exploring MQTT functionalities, MQTT Tester provides a comprehensive toolkit right on your Android device. Key Features: - Connection Setup: Easily configure MQTT connections by entering server URLs and port numbers. Optionally, you can upload and manage security certificates, ensuring secure and encrypted communication. - Subscription and Publishing: Subscribe to MQTT topics to receive real-time messages and publish messages to topics effortlessly. This feature-rich functionality allows for thorough testing of message exchange between MQTT clients and brokers. - Certificate Management: Manage and utilize SSL/TLS certificates and private keys directly within the app. This capability is crucial for establishing secure connections to MQTT brokers that require encryption. User-Friendly Interface: MQTT Tester offers an intuitive and streamlined interface.

ELIoT Responsive Garden:

ELIoT integrates plants into homes, terraces, offices, and shops by selecting the right species and simplifying cultivation. With the ELIoT App and IoT sensors, you can monitor soil moisture, temperature, and humidity in real time, receiving alerts for watering and fertilizing. Personalized care plans and voice assistant integration make plant care easy. ELIoT features automated irrigation, smart home integration for optimal lighting and climate, and a user-friendly interface with visual dashboards. Connect with a community of plant enthusiasts, access expert advice, and explore a comprehensive plant database. Advanced analytics and AI predict potential issues and track growth. With ELIoT, maintain thriving plant environments effortlessly, ensuring your plants stay healthy and vibrant.
